Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on creating machines that can perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

AI systems can learn from data, recognize patterns, understand natural language, and make decisions. Some common applications of AI include chatbots, recommendation systems, image recognition, and self-driving cars.

Machine Learning is a subset of AI that allows computers to learn from data without being explicitly programmed. Deep Learning is a more advanced type of machine learning that uses neural networks.

AI is used in many industries such as healthcare, finance, education, transportation, and entertainment.

For example, AI can help doctors diagnose diseases, help banks detect fraud, and help companies analyze large amounts of data quickly.
